Device-Local Storage

Your budget stays on your device, in this browser. We do not store your budget, transactions, or categories anywhere else.

If someone else can open this browser profile on this device, they may also be able to open your save. For better privacy, use your own device account or browser profile.

To use your budget on multiple devices, you would need to move it yourself. Right now, your save does not automatically sync between devices.

Privacy mode is active: all budget data is stored only on this device in your browser storage, with no account and no server-side budget storage.
